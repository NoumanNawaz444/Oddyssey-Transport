Our Story

We are a small, family-owned PSV (that’s Public Service Vehicle) operator based in Northern England. Like many small Bus & Coach operators, we provide a valuable service to local communities…alone you can go faster but together, we can go further!

Our aim is to make your journey Hassle-Free, convenient and most importantly, at a fair price! For your safety, all our drivers hold a valid CPC (that’s Certificate of Professional Competence) and have undergone the right training for their Category D Licence (the one for Buses & Coaches).

Oh and BTW (by the way…), Odyssey Journeys holds a Standard International Operators Licence, needed to carry 8 or more PAX (Passengers) for Hire or Reward. All our vehicles are fully insured and also go through a thorough Safety Check (every 6 weeks to be specific…)
